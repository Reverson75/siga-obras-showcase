SIGA-Obras is a modern full-stack solution for security, access, and workforce management at construction sites.
The system replaces manual processes with a centralized, real-time platform, divided into a web-based administrative dashboard and a mobile operational application.

ğŸš€ Key Features
Administrative Dashboard (Web)

Hierarchical Management: Registration of Construction Sites, Companies, and Workers with their respective relationships.

Documentation Control: Approval or rejection of worker documents, controlling access to the construction site.

Real-Time Dashboard: Visualization of KPIs (Key Performance Indicators) such as total on-site personnel, active companies, and pending documentation.

Advanced Filters: Quick search for workers by status, company, or construction site.

Operational Application (Mobile)

QR Code Access Control: Fast and secure check-in and check-out registration for gatekeepers.

On-Site Consultation: Allows supervisors to check worker information and responsible parties anywhere on the construction site.

Digital Badge: Workers can view their own QR Code, status, and time bank.
